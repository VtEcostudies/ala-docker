Acknowledgement of Indigenous Communities

The Vermont Atlas of Life acknowledges Vermont's Indigenous Communites and pays respect to their Elders, past and present. We honour and celebrate the spiritual and cultural connections of Indigenous People and the biodiversity that forms part of those connections.

The Vermont Atlas of Life (VAL) is made possible by grants and generous contributions from donors.
VAL is an implementation of Atlas of Living Australia and BioAtlas Sweden.
